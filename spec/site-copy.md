# Site copy & voice

Working reference for site-facing copy (home, about, link-unfurl embeds). Use it when refining the home/about pages later.

## Voice

Dry and informative. Concrete, plain, slightly understated. Describe what the community and site actually are; let the substance carry it.

- Not hype. No "best", no exclamation-driven energy, no "sick brackets bro".
- No invented taglines. The "Get better at draft. One set at a time." line was never real copy — it was made up and should not be reused.
- Lead with what people do here (discuss strategy, keep up with sets, draft), not with adjectives about how great it is.
