# Rules
Topic: The Dischord Server Rules!

- **Keep It Family-Friendly:** No NSFW content please!
- **Keep It Kind:** No personal attacks, misogyny, racism, LGBTQ-phobia or bigotry of any sort
- **Keep It Constructive:** Challenging each others' ideas is essential to our collective improvement, but it must be done respectfully
- **Keep It Organized:** Make sure conversations are happening in the correct channels
- **Keep it Cool:** Bad Beat Stories or Salt Posts, even framed as harmless venting, are not allowed as they are not conducive to promoting positive thinking.
- **Keep it Classy:** No disparaging comments about other community members, other communities, and/or content creators. Disagreeing with a take or having alternative, constructive takes is always ok, but demeaning or belittling opinions are not.

Clarification on bad beats: Any reviews or discussion of your draft, build, or gameplay must be centered around what you learned in a constructive manner.

This may include things like signals, key decision points in the draft, cards that wheel, patterns, curve, mana efficiency or game play decisions.

This should NOT include: salt, profanity, judgmental notions about your opponents choices, or lamenting variance or "non-games".

Failure to have good faith discussions in Discord that keep these guidelines in mind will result in a warning, followed by increasing lengths or timeout or potentially removal from the Discord.

Please notify a {moderator} in the event a member violates a rule.
